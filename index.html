Mama Care

Where every heart beat tells a story

Mama Care is a wearable fetal and maternal monitoring belt and web platform which leverages AI for early detection and safer pregnancies.

Built on Four Pillars

Accessibility

Designed for affordability and use in low-resource and peri-urban settings.

Maternal Engagement

Empowering mothers to understand and participate in their own care journey.

Early Detection

Continuous sensing enables early identification of pregnancy-related risks.

Saving Lives

Supporting timely clinical decisions to improve maternal and fetal outcomes.
